Uphold Wallet

Uphold is a big crypto trading and investing platform that lets you use the crypto wallet for a specific cryptocurrency like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, etc. You can use the Uphold wallet services by signing up for free on its official site. After creating your Uphold account, you are allowed to install the desired Uphold wallet on your device. So, if you are also one of them who are looking to set up their Uphold wallet then you have visited the right page as we are going to discuss the complete setup process of Uphold wallet on this page.

Sign up for an Uphold account

People who have not signed up for an Uphold account need to follow the steps that are given below to set up the Uphold account easily and quickly:

  1. Using a browser on your device, visit the Uphold.com site

  2. Now, find and click on the ‘uphold wallet Sign up’ button

  3. Enter the name and email address in the given fields

  4. Then, choose a password for your Uphold account

  5. Now, enter the country of residence and citizenship details

  6. Check the box next to Uphold terms and conditions

  7. Now, click on the ‘Next’ button to move on to the next page

  8. On this page, you need to verify the email address

  9. Get the verification code and enter it on this page

  10. Now, click ‘Finish’ to complete the account sign up process

Method to install Uphold wallet

If you want to install an Upload wallet then you need to follow the steps that are given below. When asked, enter the username and password of your Uphold account to proceed with the wallet downloading process.

  1. Using a browser, visit the Uphold.com webpage

  2. From this page, click on the desired wallet

  3. Now, you need to click on the ‘Get the Uphold wallet’ button

  4. On this page, Uphold asks you to sign in to your account

  5. Enter the username and password details of your Uphold account

  6. After signing in to your account, you need to click on the ‘Download’ button

  7. The downloading process of the Uphold wallet app will be started

  8. Locate the setup file on your device and follow the remaining steps to install it

How to set up the Uphold wallet?

One who has installed the Uphold wallet can set up the wallet easily by following the steps that are given below.

  1. Launch the Uphold wallet on your device

  2. Head to the homepage and click on the ‘Create Wallet’ option

  3. Now, type a new password for your account

  4. Repeat the same password in the next field

  5. Check the details and click on the ‘Next’ button

  6. Now, you will get the password recovery phrase from this page

  7. Get the phrase and note it down on a piece of paper

  8. Now, you are asked to confirm that you have found the recovery phrase

  9. Click on the ‘Next’ button to direct yourself to the next page

  10. Now, you need to enter the recovery code phrase in the required fields

  11. After feeding the secret code, click ‘Confirm’ button

  12. Finally, your Uphold wallet is ready to use
